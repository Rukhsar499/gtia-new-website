About Us

  • Home
  • About Us

George Telegraph Institute of Accounts, a division of The George Telegraph Group, prides itself on its highly qualified faculty who tirelessly strive to provide students with the finest training in vocational courses. To give students an advantage in interviews, we organize personality development and grooming sessions. Additionally, our Advisory Board is comprised of Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Financial Analysts, Business Administrators, and Cost and Management Accountants who offer essential guidance to ensure students are well-prepared for employment and confident in their skills within their chosen fields.

Established in 1920, The George Telegraph Training Institute has extensive experience in vocational education, leading to a standardized syllabus and teaching methodology that is both structured and adaptable to the evolving needs of various industries.

TThe Institute also conducts a range of employee training and assessment programs for external organizations, either on-campus or off-campus, as required.

Currently, The George Telegraph Training Institute is offering training to over 25,000 students at more than 70 centers across West Bengal, Tripura, Assam, and Odisha. The Institute partners with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), a non-profit entity under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India. All courses available at the centers are affiliated with NSDC.
