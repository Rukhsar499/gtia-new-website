why choose us

Choosing George Telegraph Institute of Accounts means benefiting from a century-long legacy of educational excellence. Our account training institute covers all aspects of accounting, guided by experienced faculty who bring real-world insights into the classroom. We emphasize hands-on training with practical sessions and internships, ensuring students gain valuable experience. Our state-of-the-art facilities for chartered accountant courses in Kolkata and access to the latest accounting software create an optimal learning environment. We offer robust career support with job placement assistance and a strong alumni network for mentorship. Flexible learning options, affordable tuition, and a commitment to student success make our accounting and taxation course the ideal choice for aspiring accountants. At George Telegraph Institute of Accounts, we prioritize your academic and career achievements.

ABOUT US

George Telegraph Institute of Accounts, a unit of The George Telegraph Group, boasts of its highly qualified team of teachers who work relentlessly towards giving students the best training possible in the account training centre with our vocational courses. Personality development and grooming sessions are organized for the students so that they have an edge when they face interviews.
