Overview

WHY EBA?

This course aims to provide you with essential insights into the realm of Accounts, where we will cover the foundational elements of Bookkeeping, Accounting, Financial Statements, and Ratio Analysis together.

Has Accounting always seemed like an unfamiliar language to you?

Do Financial Statements create such confusion in your life that you find yourself avoiding them?

If you struggle to grasp 'the numbers,' and have answered YES to ANY of these questions, then youâ€™ve come to the perfect place.

This course is designed to equip you with the crucial insights needed to navigate the World of Accounts, as we explore the fundamental aspects of Bookkeeping, Accounting, Financial Statements, and Ratio Analysis together.

KEY BENEFITS
Discover how balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements are developed and how each interact.
Evaluate the financial health of a business using financial statements.
Get opportunities of working in national and international organisations or start your own business.
Understand GAAP and IFRS standards.
Prepare and evaluate financial forecasts to make strategic decisions.
Value a venture, project, or investment opportunity and perform a sensitivity analysis.
WHAT YOU LEARN?
BUILD A SOLID FOUNDATION IN UNDERSTANDING BUSINESS by learning the Basics of Accounts.
LEARN TO READ FINANCIAL STATEMENTS to assess the health of any Business
Understand how the Income Statement, Balance Sheet and Cash Flow Statement work and tie in together.
Learn about common Entrepreneurial mistakes - such as not understanding the difference between Profits and Cash Flow which drain an Entrepreneurs Resources.
Learn practical strategies which can be used to REACH BUSINESS GOALS FASTER.
Learn 'Accounting Jargon' and 'Real Life Business Examples' making you a better Investor.
Have a complete 'Paradigm shift' in how you view business making you understand the world of Business better.
Learn the Basics of Bookkeeping and how to use free Accounting Software to manage Accounts.
Learn about RATIO ANALYSIS & FINANCIAL RATIOS to help you get quick & insightful information about companies.
