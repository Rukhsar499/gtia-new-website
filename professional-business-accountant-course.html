Overview

WHY ADPBA?

Small businesses in your area, hospitals nearby, shopping malls close by, and your favorite restaurants all share one common element: they employ accountants who analyze and interpret financial transactions to enhance their success. The Advanced Diploma in Professional Business Accountant course is a self-paced program featuring the Accounting Map through engaging tutorials. It aims to equip future students and professionals at all levels with essential knowledge in Financial Accounting, Direct and Indirect Tax, Microsoft Office, Advanced Excel, Costing and Finance, Banking and Insurance, Auditing, and more. With a career in accounting and commerce, you could find opportunities in a wide range of sectors. GTIA has created this course in response to the extensive demand for skilled professionals across various industries. Our Advanced Diploma in Professional Business Accountant program is tailored to provide you with the qualifications necessary for success, complemented by innovative placements and internships to maximize your future career prospects.

KEY BENEFITS
Secure a life-long career as an Accounting Professional.
Get comprehensive training on Accounting, Taxation, Computer Skills and Soft Skills for a complete professional development.
Get opportunities of working in national and international organisations or start your own business.
